Shree Jambu Maharishi Ashram

Shree Jambu Maharishi Ashram offers a sacred space for prayer and meditation, fostering spiritual growth in a serene environment. We provide free Meditation and Yoga sessions to enhance physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Our specialized coaching for NEET, JEE, and IAS supports students in their academic endeavors. We also offer skill development training to empower physically challenged individuals, widows, and residents of old-age homes. Join us in observing celestial events for spiritual reflection and community engagement.
