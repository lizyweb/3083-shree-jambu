ABOUT US

Shree Jambu Maharishi Ashram

The organization "SHREE JAMBU MAHARISHI ASHRAM" was established in 2022 under the Indian Trust Act of 1882. The Ashram is dedicated to providing various services and programs for the community.

SHREE JAMBU MAHARISHI ASHRAM is a non-profit organization committed to serving the community through spiritual, educational, and social initiatives.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide free access to spiritual practices, educational support, and skill development training to empower individuals from all walks of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a harmonious society where individuals can grow spiritually, intellectually, and physically.

Programmes

The Temple for Worship at SHREE JAMBU MAHARISHI ASHRAM provides a serene and sacred space for individuals to connect with their spirituality and engage in religious practices free of cost. It serves as a place for prayer, meditation, and reflection.
The Ashram offers free Meditation and Yoga practice sessions to promote physical, mental, and emotional well-being among participants. These practices help individuals reduce stress, improve concentration, and enhance overall health.
Coaching services are provided for competitive exams like NEET, JEE, and IAS to support students in their academic pursuits. The Ashram aims to empower and guide students towards achieving their educational goals through specialized coaching programs.
Skill Development Training is offered to physically challenged individuals, widows, widowers, and residents of old age orphanage homes to enhance their skills and improve their employability. These training programs aim to provide opportunities for personal growth and self-sufficiency.
Special events and programs are organized at the Ashram to observe significant celestial events like Lunar Eclipse & Solar Eclipse. These events provide a platform for spiritual reflection, learning, and community engagement.
